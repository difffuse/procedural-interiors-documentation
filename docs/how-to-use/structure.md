# Structure

Create and modify architectural structural elements including walls, columns, beams, and foundations.

## Overview

Structural tools provide professional-grade architectural elements with proper construction details, material specifications, and building code compliance.

## Wall Systems

### Wall Types
- **Interior Walls**: Non-load bearing partitions and room dividers
- **Exterior Walls**: Weather-resistant perimeter walls with insulation
- **Load-Bearing Walls**: Structural walls supporting floors and roof
- **Curtain Walls**: Non-structural glazed wall systems
- **Specialty Walls**: Fire-rated, acoustic, or security wall assemblies

### Wall Creation
1. **Draw Wall Path**: Click to define wall centerline or use existing edges
2. **Set Parameters**: Height, thickness, and construction type
3. **Choose Materials**: Structural and finish material specifications
4. **Generate Geometry**: Automatic wall creation with proper details

### Wall Parameters
- **Height**: Floor-to-ceiling or custom height dimensions
- **Thickness**: Wall assembly thickness including finishes
- **Base Height**: Raised foundation or slab-on-grade
- **Top Condition**: Ceiling attachment or parapet details

## Column and Beam Systems

### Structural Elements
- **Columns**: Vertical load-bearing supports
- **Beams**: Horizontal spanning elements
- **Girders**: Primary structural beams
- **Joists**: Secondary floor and roof framing
- **Bracing**: Lateral stability elements

### Column Types
- **Steel Columns**: W-shapes, HSS, and pipe columns
- **Concrete Columns**: Round, square, and rectangular sections
- **Wood Columns**: Solid timber and engineered lumber
- **Composite**: Steel-concrete and other hybrid systems

### Beam Specifications
- **Span Length**: Distance between supports
- **Load Capacity**: Dead and live load requirements
- **Deflection Limits**: Serviceability criteria
- **Connection Details**: Beam-to-column and beam-to-wall connections

## Foundation Systems

### Foundation Types
- **Slab-on-Grade**: Concrete slab with integrated footings
- **Crawl Space**: Raised floor with perimeter foundation walls
- **Full Basement**: Below-grade space with foundation walls
- **Pile Foundations**: Deep foundations for challenging soil conditions

### Foundation Parameters
- **Depth**: Below-grade depth for frost protection
- **Width**: Footing width for load distribution
- **Reinforcement**: Steel reinforcing bar specifications
- **Waterproofing**: Moisture protection systems

## Structural Analysis

### Load Calculations
- **Dead Loads**: Permanent structural and architectural loads
- **Live Loads**: Occupancy and equipment loads
- **Environmental Loads**: Wind, seismic, and snow loads
- **Load Combinations**: Code-required load case combinations

### Structural Validation
- **Member Sizing**: Automatic sizing based on loads and spans
- **Deflection Check**: Serviceability limit verification
- **Stress Analysis**: Allowable stress design validation
- **Connection Design**: Joint capacity and detailing

## Construction Details

### Wall Assemblies
- **Framing Details**: Stud spacing, headers, and blocking
- **Insulation Systems**: Thermal and acoustic insulation
- **Vapor Barriers**: Moisture control and air sealing
- **Finish Systems**: Drywall, plaster, and other wall finishes

### Structural Connections
- **Welded Connections**: Steel-to-steel welded joints
- **Bolted Connections**: High-strength bolt assemblies
- **Cast-in-Place**: Concrete connection details
- **Mechanical Fasteners**: Screws, nails, and specialty fasteners

## Material Specifications

### Structural Materials
- **Steel**: Grade specifications and section properties
- **Concrete**: Strength, mix design, and reinforcement
- **Wood**: Species, grade, and engineered lumber products
- **Masonry**: Brick, block, and stone specifications

### Material Properties
- **Strength Values**: Allowable stresses and design values
- **Durability**: Corrosion resistance and weathering
- **Sustainability**: Recycled content and environmental impact
- **Cost Factors**: Material and installation cost considerations

## Advanced Structural Features

### Parametric Structures
- **Adaptive Sizing**: Automatic member sizing based on loads
- **Optimization**: Minimum weight or cost optimization
- **Alternative Analysis**: Compare different structural systems
- **Sensitivity Studies**: Parameter variation analysis

### Structural Modifications
- **Member Replacement**: Change structural element types
- **System Conversion**: Convert between structural systems
- **Reinforcement**: Add structural reinforcement elements
- **Demolition Planning**: Selective structural removal

### Integration with Other Systems
- **MEP Coordination**: Structural openings for utilities
- **Architectural Integration**: Structure and architecture coordination
- **Construction Sequencing**: Phased construction planning
- **Cost Estimation**: Structural cost analysis and budgeting

## Structural Workflows

### Design Development Process
1. **Conceptual Design**: Basic structural layout and sizing
2. **Load Analysis**: Detailed load calculations and combinations
3. **Member Design**: Structural element sizing and selection
4. **Connection Design**: Joint design and detailing
5. **Construction Documents**: Detailed drawings and specifications

### Structural Coordination
1. **Architectural Coordination**: Structure and architecture integration
2. **MEP Coordination**: Utility routing and structural openings
3. **Construction Coordination**: Buildability and sequencing
4. **Code Compliance**: Building code and standard compliance

### Building Codes
- **International Building Code (IBC)**: General building requirements
- **International Residential Code (IRC)**: Residential construction
- **ASCE 7**: Minimum design loads for buildings
- **Local Amendments**: Regional code modifications

### Design Standards
- **AISC Steel Construction Manual**: Steel design standards
- **ACI Building Code**: Concrete design requirements
- **NDS Wood Construction**: Wood design specifications
- **TMS Masonry Standards**: Masonry design and construction
